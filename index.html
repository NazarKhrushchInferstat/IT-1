BERLIN

Berlin is the capital and most largest cy in Germany. Institutionally, it is a city- state named Land of Berlin .Located in the north-east of the country, Berlin has around 3.8 million inhabitants. Its inhabitants are called Berliners and Berliners ( die Berliner and die Berlinerinnen in German ). It is the first city and the eighth most populous agglomeration in the European Union . The greater Berlin area covers 892 km 2 with a population of 4.4 million .
